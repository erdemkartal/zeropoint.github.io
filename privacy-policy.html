Privacy Policy

1. Data Controller

The developer of the ZeroPoint app, Erdem Kartal ("we"), is the data controller under this policy. Contact details are provided at the end of this document, in the app's "About" section and on the Google Play store listing.

2. Data We Process

  • Game data: scores, streaks, difficulty, language and settings preferences.
  • Device data: device model, OS version, app version and general diagnostics.
  • Advertising ID: processed by Google AdMob for ad serving and ad-fraud prevention.
  • Purchase status: whether "Remove Ads" is active. We never access your payment details; payments are processed by Google Play.
  • If you sign in with Google: your email address, display name and Firebase user ID.

3. Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Performance of contract: providing the game, cloud save, purchases and restore.
  • Consent: personalized advertising (collected via Google's consent form — UMP — in the EEA/UK).
  • Legitimate interest: security, debugging, abuse prevention and non-personalized ads where permitted.

4. Advertising and Consent

The app shows ads via Google AdMob. Users in the EEA and UK are asked for consent through Google's UMP consent form. Without consent, non-personalized or limited ads may be shown. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via device settings or the in-app consent form; withdrawal does not affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

Your data is shared only with these processors: Google Play Services, Google Play Billing, Google AdMob and Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, App Check). These services are governed by Google's privacy policy.

6. International Data Transfers

Data may be processed on Google servers outside your country. Google applies appropriate safeguards such as the EU Standard Contractual Clauses.

7. Retention

Local data stays on your device until you uninstall the app or reset your data. Cloud data is kept while your account is active. Leaderboard entries are kept while the service operates. Upon a deletion request, your data is erased within a reasonable time.

8. Your Rights (GDPR Art. 15–21)

You have the right to access, rectify, erase (right to be forgotten), restrict processing, data portability, object, and withdraw any consent you have given. You may also lodge a complaint with your supervisory data protection authority. Use the contact details below for requests.

9. Children's Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the applicable digital consent age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from this age group; if discovered, such data is deleted immediately.

10. Security

Industry-standard technical and organizational measures protect your data. However, no transmission or storage method is 100% secure.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated. For material changes, you will be asked to consent again inside the app. The current version is always published in the app and on this page.
